 Interview Committee:
This practice is very common and uselful in ensuring diversity. The concept is that using a board of diverse
executives to attend and run interviews is better than a single person running the interview. In this way, a single
 person's bias will not come into play to the same extent as it would with one interviewer.

 Interview Guide:
This practice involves the use of a manual that instructs the interviewer(s) on how to manage the interview, and also 
details how to avoid bias during the process.
